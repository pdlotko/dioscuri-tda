Davide Gurnari

I was a doctoral student in the Centre from October 2020 to April 2025, working on distributed computations of persistence curves and extensions of mapper-type algorithms. I completed my PhD in April 2025 and I am now working as a postdoctoral researcher. You can learn more about me and my work by visiting my personal webpage.
