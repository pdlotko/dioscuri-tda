Niklas Hellmer

I completed my PhD in February 2025. Here you can find my thesis "Probability meets Topological Data Analysis", which is concerned with metrics for persistence diagrams and modules, Dowker complexes and interactions of TDA with statistics. Watch my AATRN seminar talk: While I have left acadmia for a position as IT consultant focused on data engineering, I'm still happy to chat about research.
